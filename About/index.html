About

Hi my name is Dr. Mary McKinnon. I am currently a physical therapist at the Damany Center for Chronic Pain and Holistic Well-being in Allentown, PA. I have extensive experience in successfully treating patients with chronic pain, repetitive strain/stress injuries (RSI), and general orthopedic conditions. I strive to treat patients with a holistic approach and appreciate the importance of hands-on, comprehensive care, all while applying evidence-based treatments.

Education

I earned a B.S. degree from the Pennsylvania State University in Kinesiology (study of human movement) in 2012.
