About

Hi my name is Dr. Mary McKinnon. I am currently a physical therapist at the Damany Center for Chronic Pain and Holistic Well-being in Allentown, PA, a world-renowned clinic for treating chronic pain and repetitive strain conditions.

I have extensive experience in successfully treating patients with chronic pain, repetitive strain/stress injuries (RSI), and general orthopedic conditions. I strive to treat patients with a holistic approach and appreciate the importance of hands-on, comprehensive care, all while applying evidence-based treatments. My ability to make a difference in patients' lives through my knowledge and skills is what motivates me to stay current on the latest physical therapy research
